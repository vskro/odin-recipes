Chicken Wings, Yet Villainous!

This image used with Creative Commons from Stu Spivack on Flickr.

Description

Wings of a chicken; baked then sauced to taste with a special twist on traditional buffalo styling.

We are not responsible for consumption of this recipe, should you should choose to do so.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen chicken wings
  • Frank's Red Hot sauce
  • Sweet Baby Ray's barbecue sauce
  • Eye of Newt

Step-by-Step:

  1. Wrap sheetpan in foil
  2. Grease pan with Pam spray
  3. Add 8-12 frozen wings to pan
  4. Bake on 450 for 60 minutes
  5. Remove wings from oven. If they appear lightly browned, they are ready to be sauced
  6. Mix 1:1 ratio of Sweet Baby Ray's and Frank's Red Hot sauce in an evil cauldron
  7. Add dash of Eye of Newt
  8. Pour mixed sauce onto cooked wings
  9. Bake sauced wings at 425 for 20-30 minutes
  10. Let cool and enjoy!
